Privacy Policy

AI Chat QuickExport — Last updated: May 19, 2026

What data we collect

None. AI Chat QuickExport does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data.

How the extension works

When you click export, the extension reads the current conversation from the AI platform you are viewing (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or Grok). The conversation data is processed entirely within your browser and saved as a file to your local computer. No data is sent to any external server.

Authentication tokens

To access your full conversation history, the extension temporarily captures authentication tokens from the AI platform's existing browser session. These tokens are held in browser memory only for the duration of the export request and are never stored to disk, transmitted externally, or logged.

Permissions used

activeTab — To read the current page URL and detect which AI platform you are on.

scripting — To capture authentication tokens from the AI platform's session, needed to fetch full conversation history.

storage — To save your speaker label preferences (e.g., custom names for user and assistant) locally in Chrome's sync storage.

downloads — To save the exported conversation file to your computer with a save-as dialog.

Host permissions (chatgpt.com, claude.ai, gemini.google.com, grok.com) — Required to access conversation data on these specific platforms only.

Third-party services

None. The extension makes no network requests to any server other than the AI platform you are actively viewing.

Data retention

The extension retains only your speaker label preferences in Chrome's local storage. No conversation data is retained after export.

Changes to this policy

Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
